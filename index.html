Charges vary depending on the academic year a student was first enrolled. However, the UC Tuition Stability Plan guarantees that Tuition, the Student Services Fee, and undergraduate Nonresident Supplemental Tuition will remain the same until that student graduates.

Tuition

Tuition pays for the core services provided by UC Berkeley to its students. The charge for every tuition type is standardized across all campuses that are part of the UC system.

Like tuition, the SSF is standardized across all UC campuses.
